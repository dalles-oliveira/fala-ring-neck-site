Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

The Fala Ring Neck app respects user privacy and aims to handle data transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app works, what information may be processed, and how that information is used.

1. About the app

Fala Ring Neck is an app designed to support the training of birds capable of learning sounds, words, songs, and whistles, allowing automatic audio playback, recording of new phrases, file import, and training organization.

2. What data may be processed

The app may process the following types of information:

  • audio recorded by the user within the app
  • audio files imported by the user
  • app settings and preferences stored locally on the device
  • identifiers related to ad delivery by third-party services, when applicable

The app does not require account registration for basic use.

3. How the data is used

The data processed by the app is used to:

  • allow recording and playback of phrases and sounds
  • import audio chosen by the user
  • organize the phrase library
  • save preferences such as language, default times, and default training mode
  • display ads, when monetization is active

4. Data storage

Recorded or imported audio files and app preferences may be stored locally on the user's device.

This information is used for the normal operation of the app and to keep the experience configured by the user.

5. Permissions used

At this time, the app may request the following permissions on Android:

  • Microphone: used to record new phrases and sounds inside the app.
  • Internet: used for features that depend on connectivity, including advertising services when active.

6. Ads and third-party services

The app may use third-party services to display ads, including Google AdMob.

These services may collect device identifiers, technical usage information, and data necessary to deliver, measure, and improve ads, according to the policies and practices of their respective providers.

For more information, users may consult the Google Privacy Policy.

7. Data sharing

Fala Ring Neck does not sell audio recorded by the user.

However, technical data and identifiers required for advertising and integrated services may be processed by third-party providers according to their own policies.

8. User control

Users may, at any time:

  • delete audio created or imported in the app
  • uninstall the app from the device
  • stop using recording features and deny microphone permission, noting that this may limit some functions

9. Audience and responsible use

The app was developed as a support tool for audio repetition training.

Use of the app should respect the bird's well-being, the animal's adaptation time, and the responsibility of the owner or caregiver.

10. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect app improvements, technical changes, new integrations, or legal and platform requirements.

When relevant changes are made, the updated version will take effect from the new revision date shown at the top of this document.
